Are you looking for a fun and challenging after-school activity for your teenager?

Look no further than CC's Active Teens 4 Success After-School Program!

Our program offers classes in a variety of striking and grappling martial arts, such as boxing, kickboxing, wrestling, and Brazilian jiu-jitsu. Our classes are taught by certified and experienced instructors who are passionate about these martial arts.

Our classes are designed to be fun and engaging, and to provide a positive and supportive learning environment for the students.

Our instructors use a variety of techniques and activities to teach the students the basic techniques and principles of these martial arts, and to help them develop their skills and confidence.

CC's Active Teens 4 Success After-School Program is open to teenagers in the age range of 12 to 18 years old.

Our classes are divided into groups based on the age and skill level of the students, in order to provide a personalized and appropriate learning experience for each student.

Our classes are held on weekday evenings, typically from 5pm to 6:30pm.

This schedule allows students to attend the classes after school, and provides an alternative to other after-school activities such as sports or clubs.

In addition to the regular classes, CC's Active Teens 4 Success After-School Program offers additional activities and services that enhance the students' learning experience and provide additional value to the parents.

These could include workshops, seminars, and camps, as well as opportunities to participate in competitions and other activities.
