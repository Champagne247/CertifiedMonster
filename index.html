Martial Arts Journey

Exploring a lifetime dedication to martial arts.

As a third-generation martial artist, I began my journey in submission wrestling under my father's tutelage. I later delved into ninjutsu and taekwondo before embracing Brazilian jiu-jitsu alongside legends Vitor Belfort and Rodrigo Medeiros.

Expanding my skills, I ventured into personal training and martial arts coaching. As the owner of LA MMA, Los Angeles's Ultimate Mixed Martial Arts Training Center, I lead the ZEROMERCY Fight Team, fostering a community of dedicated fighters and enthusiasts.

My path ultimately led me to owning a private label supplement line. It was a privilege to have my supplements in the hands of influential figures like Eddie Bravo and Joe Rogan.
